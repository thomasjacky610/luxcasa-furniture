Our Story

Crafting Beautiful Spaces Since 2010

LuxeCasa was born from a simple belief — everyone deserves a home they love. Founded in Columbus, Ohio by furniture designer Marco Barretti, we started as a small workshop and have grown into one of America's most trusted furniture brands.

Every piece we create goes through a rigorous design process rooted in sustainability, quality, and timeless aesthetics. We source our materials responsibly and work with skilled craftspeople who share our passion for detail.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you buy

How long does delivery take? +

Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Custom and bespoke orders may take 2–4 weeks. We'll keep you updated at every stage via email and SMS.

Do you offer assembly services? +

Yes! Our white-glove delivery team will assemble and position your furniture free of charge on all orders over $500. For smaller orders, assembly is available for a small fee.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days of delivery and we'll arrange a free collection and full refund.

Can I customise my order? +

Absolutely. Many of our products are available in custom sizes, fabrics, and finishes. Use the product page options or contact our team for fully bespoke requests.

Do you offer financing? +

Yes, we offer 0% interest financing on orders over $999. Apply at checkout in seconds with no impact to your credit score for checking eligibility.

What warranty do your products carry? +

All products come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty. Some premium collections carry a 5-year extended warranty. Details are listed on each product page.
