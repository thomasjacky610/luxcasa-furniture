What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or install malware on your device. They simply store a small amount of data that helps us remember you and your preferences when you visit our site.

There are two main types of cookies based on how long they last:

  • Session cookies — These are temporary and are deleted from your device when you close your browser. They allow our website to remember what you put in your shopping cart during a single browsing session.
  • Persistent cookies — These remain on your device for a set period of time (or until you delete them). They help us recognise you when you return to our website and remember your preferences.

ℹ You can control and manage cookies in your browser at any time. See the Managing cookies in your browser section below for instructions.

How we use cookies

LuxeCasa uses cookies for a number of important reasons. Cookies help us deliver a better, faster, and more personalised experience when you browse our website. Specifically, we use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account across pages
  • Remember items you've added to your shopping cart
  • Remember your preferences (such as currency, language, or delivery region)
  • Understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns and Google Ads
  • Show you relevant products and personalised recommendations
  • Prevent fraud and keep our website secure
  • Enable social media features such as sharing and reviews

We only use cookie data to improve your experience — we do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by trusted third-party services that we use to improve our site and marketing. These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices, and we encourage you to review them.

  • Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Google Ads (formerly AdWords) — Conversion tracking cookies help us understand which ads lead to purchases on our site. This data is used only for advertising performance measurement.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook) — Used for Facebook and Instagram advertising, allowing us to measure ad effectiveness and retarget visitors who have shown interest in our products.

⚠ Third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of those third parties. LuxeCasa does not control these cookies and is not responsible for the content of third-party privacy policies.

Managing cookies in your browser

In addition to the preference centre above, you can control cookies directly through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies have been set and delete them individually
  • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set
  • Browse in private/incognito mode to prevent cookies being stored

Here's how to manage cookies in the most popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Opera — Settings → Advanced → Privacy & security → Site Settings → Cookies

⚠ Please note that if you disable or delete cookies, some features of our website may not work correctly. For example, your shopping cart may not save items between visits, or you may be logged out automatically.
